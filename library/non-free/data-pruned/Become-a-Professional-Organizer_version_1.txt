Choose what type of organizing you will be doing.
There are many different types of organizing which can be divided into three main areas: home, office or life.
Home organizing usually involves decluttering and creating storage solutions.
Office organizing usually involves decluttering and setting up workspaces, and creating filing and data systems.
Life organizing usually involves helping people learn to prioritize, set goals, and manage their time.
Obtain training.
Training is optional, but there are lots of courses or seminars you can attend to hone your organizing abilities.
