Click on Start.
Type CMD and press enter to open the Command prompt.
Type VOL C : and press enter.
This will display your hard drive's label (if applicable) and serial number.
Take note of these, as you will need them to convert the drive.
Type CONVERT C : /FS:NTFS and press enter.
Enter the requested information that you retained from above.
