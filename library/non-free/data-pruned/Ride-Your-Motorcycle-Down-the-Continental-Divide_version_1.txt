ride..Join the Yahoo discussion group "Great Divide Ride" at : http://groups.yahoo.com/group/GreatDivideRide/
Visit the Adventure Cycling Association at : http://www.adventurecycling.org/routes/greatdivide.cfm
Find a buddy with a similar bike and skills to join you.
Prep your bike for two weeks of adventure off highway either camping each night or utilizing the many motels and B&B's noted on the route maps.
Decide if you will ride or ship your bike to the start and finish points.
Block out 2-3 weeks vacation time.
