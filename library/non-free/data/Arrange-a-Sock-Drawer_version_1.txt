around.Take out all of your socks from the drawer.
Lay them out on a clean surface, such as a bed or table.
Spread them out so you can see every single sock.
Sort the socks by color.
Find pairs of socks.
Place two matching socks together on top of each other.
White socks can usually be placed with similar white ones, even if they aren't exact matches.
Keep the pairs together.
You can either fold the pair in half or fold one of the cuffs around both socks.
Alternatively, you can just keep them flat together.
