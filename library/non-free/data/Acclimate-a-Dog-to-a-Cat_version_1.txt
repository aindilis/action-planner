spacePrepare the dog for the new arrival by giving it items that belong to the cat to sniff and become acquainted with its scent.
Place the cat in a carrier to keep it safe as you acclimate the dog to the cat.
Create a safe, clutter-free environment to introduce the dog to the cat.
Erect a barrier such as a baby gate, allowing them to sniff each other through the barrier.
Keep the dog on a leash when acclimating it to a cat, holding it firmly.
Interrupt any unacceptable behavior by moving the dog away from the cat, and redirecting both animals' attention.
Teach the dog a command like "leave it" or "leave alone" and use it whenever you want the dog to leave the cat alone.
Praise the cat when it is gentle around the dog and praise the dog when it is calm around the cat.
